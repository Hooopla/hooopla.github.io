Hi, my name is Gabriel

I am currently a student at Simon Fraser University and an aspiring software developer.

My goal is to build a career in software development, creating impactful applications that enhance and simplify people’s everyday lives.

How and why did I start coding?

Inspired by the theme of this website, my programming journey began with Minecraft. At just 10 years old, I started experimenting with the game’s in-built mechanics, using them to create interactive elements and simple games. This early curiosity eventually led me to develop custom servers, leveraging only Minecraft’s native tools. By high school, I discovered Skript—a powerful plugin that enables developers to extend and customize gameplay—expanding my ability to bring my ideas to life, from designing a fishing-based server to building a fully immersive MMORPG.

Reflecting on these experiences, I’m grateful for the foundation they laid in my childhood, sparking my ambition to pursue Software Engineering. Today, I’m driven by the vision of creating software that makes a meaningful impact in people’s lives.
